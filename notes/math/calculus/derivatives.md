# Derivatives

1. Three rules of derivative.

    * Constant rule: $f'(x) = 0$
 
    * Sum rule: $(\alpha f(x)+ \beta g(x))' = \alpha f'(x) + \beta g'(x)$
 
    * Product rule: $(f(x)g(x))' = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)$
 
    * Quotient rule: $\left({\frac {f(x)}{g(x)}}\right)'={\frac {f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g^{2}(x)}}$
 
    * Chain rule: $(f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x))g'(x)$
